## What is Celcoin?

[](https://developers.celcoin.com.br/docs#o-que-%C3%A9-a-celcoin)

Celcoin, founded in 2016, is a financial infratech to boost businesses.

Through cel_bricks, its Embedded Finance solution, it provides complete technology infrastructure for regulated and non-regulated companies, from the most diverse segments, and provides modular solutions for banking, credit, Open Finance, cash management, retail, onboarding and issuance of card.

A pioneer in the market, Celcoin is also at the forefront of yet another transformation in the segment with mybenk: a complete banking solution for all stakeholders in an ecosystem. A bank with Endofinance!

Estão conectadas à plataforma da Celcoin cerca de 600 bancos digitais, unicórnios e fintechs, mais de 6 mil médias e grandes empresas e 40 mil pontos de varejo, que, juntos, processam mensalmente mais de R$ 40 bilhões.

Its recognition also lies in achievements such as awards for “Best Startup in Brazil” by Seedstars, “Best Fintech in the Country” by BBVA Open Talent, “The Inclusive Fintech 50”, “100 Startups to Watch” by Pequenas Empresas & Grandes Negócios, contribution financial support from Summit Partners with participation from Innova Capital, as well as “Great Place to Work (GPTW)” and “Company B” certificates. It was also one of the big winners of the “Banking Transformation Award”, organized by Cantarino Brasileiro, in the Open Banking category.

To strengthen its team and its scope of action, it also acquired the companies Galax Pay, focused on recurring billing solutions, Flow Finance, which provides credit infrastructure, Finansystech, a leading startup in Open Finance solutions, and Regulatório+, of solutions regulatory.

Whatever your segment of activity, it is simple to offer financial and banking services to your customers. This is Celcoin. The best service for growth.
